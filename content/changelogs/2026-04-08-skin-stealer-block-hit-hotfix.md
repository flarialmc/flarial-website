# HOTFIX

## Changes

- Fixed `Skin Stealer` not saving skins in the correct directory.
  - Cloned skins can now be found in `%localappdata%\Flarial\Client\Skins`.
- Added trigger modes to `Block Hit`.
  - The animation can now be triggered by either mouse button, or a combination of both.
