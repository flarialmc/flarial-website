# BETA DLL NOTICE

## Changes

We have decided to make some changes to how beta DLLs are distributed.

- The launcher will no longer provide an option to use the beta DLL.
- Instead, you will only have the option to use the stable DLL of the client or a custom DLL.

## How do I get beta DLLs then?

To get beta DLLs and provide feedback on them, consider joining our tester program (see `announcements` in Discord).

Please let us know if there are any issues via our [GitHub Issue Tracker](https://github.com/flarialmc/dll/issues) or community support.
